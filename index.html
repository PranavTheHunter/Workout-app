Beginner's Guide

Warm Up First

Never skip warm-ups. 5-10 minutes of light cardio or dynamic stretching prepares your muscles and prevents injury.

Hydration

Drink water before, during, and after your workout. Muscles need hydration to recover and perform.

Rest Days

Muscles grow while you sleep, not while you lift. Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep and take rest days.

Progressive Overload

To grow, you must increase difficulty over time. Add more weight, reps, or sets gradually.
